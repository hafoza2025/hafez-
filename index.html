Biography

Professional background and expertise

Mohamed El-Hafez is an Egyptian software developer and technology entrepreneur specializing in artificial intelligence and software development. He began his programming journey in 2022 through self-learning, and within three years mastered web development, database technologies, and artificial intelligence.

In 2024, at the age of 23, he founded Meta Software with a clear vision to integrate AI technologies into practical business solutions. The company achieved a remarkable market valuation of 25 million EGP within just 5 months of establishment, expanding the team to over 25 specialists serving clients throughout Egypt and the Arab region.
